Brands and patents solution


Software designed for Suprema, a company specialized in the registration of trademarks and patents. To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted confidential information in this case study.




Discover


The software project was designed to facilitate, organize and automate the processes related to trademark and patent registration, making employee communication faster and in a more efficient way.

The software user interface and user experience were designed based on user needs, respecting the brand identity and making navigation intuitive.

Workshops and user interviews were performed during field research immersive into the users' workstation. These activities helped us to understand how people behave in the real work context.
