Inlog Help Center


Project that was conducted during my work at Inlog.

Help center platform where users can find help to use a complex tracking and transportation system.



The Inlog Group is a software and hardware company that produces solutions for tracking and vehicle operations management, bus transportation and waste collection segments.

In this project, we identified the difficulties that the users face while using the complex software and proposed a solution that generated value both for the consultants who implement the software and for the operators who work directly with the Inlog's solutions. We followed Google Design Sprint steps.

01. UNDERSTAND
Value Proposition Canvas

The Value Proposition Canvas was used as a support to understand what was the consulting user's pains and what they are expecting to solve their problems. It was important to focus on solutions that bring value for the consultors and users.


User Interviews - Quantitative and Qualitative Research

User interviews were conducted with transport operators, support and implementation consultors where they started to complain about the issues and difficulties to use the digital products.

We performed researches thought forms sent by e-mail to evaluate the difficulty level to use each type of software. It helped us to understand which point we will prioritize in our activities and what kind of difficulties the users are facing.

We discovered that some users have no idea how to use some applications, and they have never used them because of that.



Competitive analysis

A competitive analysis was performed to explore similar products, map the most common market features and to discover new tendencies and the patterns related.


02. DEFINE
Experience View


Based on the collected data, we used a tool called Experience View as a summary to show how was the user experience while using the products.

We notice that as the users engage the product, they start to need help to use that because the workflow starts to be more complex and difficult to complete the tasks.


03. DIVERGE
Brainstorming | Wireframes


Wireframes were built and cropped in pieces to perform a brainstorming session with the users where they will organize the information according to their mental model.


04. DECIDE
Voting for the best idea


The best solution was choosen to be developed.


05. PROTOTYPE
Help Center visual interface

The next step was to build an MVP of the solution in Wordpress to write content related to how to use the applications and features.

The purpose of choosing the Wordpress platform was that will be easier to feed the content and to do maintenance without a developer skill.
