Features Lab


Linx Impulse is a software company that provides personalized customers' experiences in e-commerce. I had the challenge to evolve the current releases page to deliver more value to the users.


Since the acquisition in 2015, the products alongside the growth of the company. The number of new features released increased, and the users did not have visibility about the evolution of the products.

To better understand the problem we analyzed NPS results, interviewed some users to comprehend better their needs and executed a competitive analysis.

The data collected is properly documented to facilitate analysis of results, such as identifying patterns among responses.
